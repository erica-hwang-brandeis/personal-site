Design Project 1

As the first hands-on activity in the course, this project introduces the fundamentals of 3D printing, covering CAD, CAM, and the workflow from slicing to final print. Through calibration prints and a "torture test," it explores the capabilities and limitations of 3D design, laying the foundation for more advanced fabrication techniques.
